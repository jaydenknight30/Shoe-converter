Help Center

Common questions about international shoe sizing.

Is there a difference between US Men's and Women's sizes?

Yes. Typically, there is a 1.5-size difference. A US Women's 8.5 is roughly equal to a US Men's 7.

What does "EU Sizing" actually mean?

European sizing is based on "Paris Points," where each point is 2/3 of a centimeter. This is why EU sizes are higher numbers (e.g., 42, 43).

Why do my Nike shoes feel smaller than my Adidas?

Different brands use different molds. Nike often runs slightly narrower, while Adidas can run wider. Check the JP/CM size for the best comparison.

What is the "JP" or "CM" size?

This represents the actual length of your foot in centimeters. It is the most consistent measurement across all global brands.
