Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about international shoe sizing and how to find your perfect fit.

Is there a difference between US Men's and Women's sizes?

Yes. Typically, there is a 1.5-size difference. A US Women's 8.5 is roughly equal to a US Men's 7. This tool accounts for those differences based on the standard conversion charts.

What does "EU Sizing" actually mean?

European sizing is based on "Paris Points," where each point is 2/3 of a centimeter. This is why EU sizes (like 42 or 43) are much higher numbers than US or UK sizes.

Why do my Nike shoes feel smaller than my Adidas?

Different brands use different "lasts" (the foot-shaped molds used to make the shoe). Nike often runs slightly narrower, while Adidas can run a bit wider. Always check the JP/CM size for the most accurate brand-to-brand comparison.

What is the "JP" or "CM" size?

This is the most accurate measurement as it represents the literal length of the foot in centimeters. If you know your foot length in CM, you can find your size in any country's system.
